Android Beta Access Instructions

To get started with the SoPets beta on your Android device:

  1. You'll receive an email from Google Play's Beta Testing program
  2. Click the "Join Beta" button in that email
  3. Visit the SoPets Play Store page to download the beta version
  4. Sign in with the same email you used to register
Join Beta on Google Play

Note: The beta version will replace any existing SoPets app installation. You can opt out of the beta at any time through the Play Store.
